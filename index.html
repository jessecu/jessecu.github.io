About Me

Hello! I am Jie Xu, a 3rd-year PhD candidate at the Department of Computer Science, City University of Hong Kong. I am supervised by Prof. Xiaohua Jia (IEEE Fellow).

In 2020, I received my master's degree in Electronics and Communication Engineering from the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC), where I was supervised by Prof. Kaiping Xue and Prof. Peilin Hong .

I have had the fortune of collaborating with Prof. Cong Wang (IEEE Fellow).
