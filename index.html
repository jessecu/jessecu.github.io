Biography

I am a 1st year PhD student at the Department of Computer Science, City University of Hong Kong. My supervisor is Prof. Xiaohua Jia (IEEE Fellow).

I received my Master degree in Electronices and Communication Engineering from the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in 2020, supervised by Prof. Peiling Hong and Prof. Kaiping Xue.
I received my degree of Bachelor of Engineering in Information Security and degree of Bachelor of Literature in Communication (Dual) from the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in 2017.

My reseach interests include the cyber security, privacy preservation, computer security and applied cryptography.

I've had the good fortune of working with some talented people Peixuan He and Huancheng Zhou.
